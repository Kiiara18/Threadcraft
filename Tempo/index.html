About

Learn More About Us

We are Kenyan-based designers dedicated to making all of your crotchet and knitting dreams come true.

Our aim is to be a brand that embodies economical and environmentally-safe products. All of our products are:

  • Made with eco-friendly thread.
  • Durable and do not wear with time.
  • Handmade with love.

Our company has partnered with various organizations to ensure that our products are sourced economically and that our factories are maintaining high standards of employee care.
